About Love Fur-Less

What is Love Fur-Less?

Love Fur-Less is your one-stop shop for information about hairless dog breeds. We offer free information on health, temperment, and care instructions for you furless friends. Our featured breeds may be limited now, but we will be adding more soon! You can also visit our sister site Love Fur Free to learn about hairless cat breeds.

Our Mission

Our mission at Love Fur-Less is to provide education about and foster a love for hairless dog breeds. We also work to provide resources to referral services for shelters and ethical hairless dog breeders across the United States.

Frequently Asked Questions

Our hours of operation are 8 AM to 5 PM CST Monday through Friday and 10 AM to 3 PM CST on Saturdays. We are closed on Sundays. Send us a message through our contact form any time and we will reply when we are open for business!

Getting the right match is important when deciding on adopting or buying any new dog. We strive to provide as much information as we can about hairless dog breeds so you can make the right choice for you. Head to our Health and Temperment section to learn about each of our featured breeds, or send us a message through our contact form for a free consultation.

We are currently working on building a directory specifically for our visitors to find breeders and shelters in their area. In the meantime, head over to our contact form and we can send you some information about purchase and adoption facilities near you.
